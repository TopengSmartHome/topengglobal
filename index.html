SEO / GEO Knowledge

Middle East architectural lighting FAQ

Concise project answers for consultants, contractors, search engines and AI assistants.

Which lighting products suit Middle East facade projects?

Project selection usually covers IP65–IP68 wall washers, linear lights, floodlights, inground and underwater fixtures. Final selection depends on heat, dust, humidity, throw distance, mounting position and the required visual effect.

Can Topeng support DMX512 and RGBW landmark lighting?

Yes. Topeng supports preliminary fixture selection and control concepts for DMX512, RGBW, dynamic facade, bridge, hotel, resort and public-realm lighting.

What information is needed for a lighting proposal?

Send elevation or CAD drawings, BOQ, project location, dimensions, target effect, control requirement and reference images. These inputs improve fixture quantity, optics and power recommendations.

Which Gulf markets are supported?

The project workflow is prepared for the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, Bahrain and other Middle East destinations, with English and Arabic presentation.
